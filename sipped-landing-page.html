What it does

Your nights deserve a highlight reel.

Every great night out is a story. Sipped gives it the platform it deserves.

🌃

Sessions

Start a Session when the night begins. Name it, log drinks, check in to venues. End the night with a shareable recap card — your personal highlight reel.

👑

The Regular

Every bar has a leaderboard. The person with the most check-ins in 90 days earns The Regular crown. It's yours to claim — and yours to lose.

🏴

Territory

Every neighborhood has an owner. Check in across a district and climb the Zone leaderboard. The Territory Map shows who rules your city in real time.

👥

Crews

Form a Crew with your friends and compete together. Crew leaderboards, shared feeds, and Crew vs. Crew rivalries. Going out has never been more competitive.

🗺️

Crawls

Plan a bar crawl route, share it publicly, and let others run it too. Every Crawl has its own leaderboard. Classic Crawls in your city get a verified badge.

🥂

Toasts

One reaction. Always positive. Like Strava's Kudos — but for the bar stool. No dislikes. No drama. Just a culture of celebrating great nights.

How it works

Dead simple. Wildly addictive.

Four steps from your couch to owning your city's nightlife.

1

Start a Session

Name your night. Pick your Session type. Hit go. The timer starts — your legend begins.

2

3
